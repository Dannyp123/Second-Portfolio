Dunn's Country Store

I currently work at a gas station / convenient store / fishing and hunting-shop named Dunn's Country Store. It is owned and operated by Gene and Loretta Dunn. I have been working there for almost two years now. I do pretty much everything. Although, I am mostly a waiter, I also cook, clean, stock, and deal with live bait.I also handle the money in the store. This means that I am trustworthy. I enjoy what I do while at work. It is full of great people and wonderful hospitality.
